How A Batch Moves

Five stages, tracked ticket to truck.

Every batch is logged at each stage — the number on your ticket traces back to the raw materials that went into it.

01
Batching
Aggregate, cement, and admixtures weighed to the mix design.
02
Molding
Cast in steel forms under vibration for full compaction.
03
Curing
Controlled humidity and temperature for 24–28 days.
04
QC Testing
Compressive strength and dimensional checks per lot.
05
Delivery
Palletized and trucked on a scheduled dispatch window.
